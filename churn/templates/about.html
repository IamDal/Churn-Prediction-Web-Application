About Churn Predictor

The web app was designed to help marketing and retention teams within financial institutions accurately and efficiently identify members who may be likely to leave or seek out other service providers.
The dataset on which the model was trained, is hosted on kaggle and is a synthetic data of over 10 thousand records.

Using Churn Predictor

There are two methods to using the web app.
The first method is uploading your very own csv, formatted with the columns as shown on the form. Once uploaded the predictions will be made in the background and results automatically downloaded to the end users PC. The csv will be updated with churn probability as an added column.

The other is to enter a single customer data. This is a quick and easy way to instantly check an individual customer by filling out the form. The results being displayed as a flash message.

How to use (uploading csv)

Select your csv file then click download. It's that easy!
